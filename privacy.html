Privacy Policy

How Alafia Braiding Studio handles client information.

When you use the booking form, the studio may receive your name, email address, phone number, requested service, preferred date and time, and any style notes you provide.

This information is used only to review booking requests, respond to appointment questions, and communicate about salon services. The site may also store your draft request in your browser using local storage so you do not lose your progress before submission.

Live booking submissions are currently delivered through FormSubmit to the salon email inbox. If you prefer, you can contact the salon directly by phone, email, or Instagram instead of using the form.

For questions about your information, contact alafiabraidingsalon@gmail.com.
