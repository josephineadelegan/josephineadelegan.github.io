FAQ

Helpful details before you book.

Do I need to come with my hair washed and blown out?

Yes. Clean, stretched hair is preferred unless you request prep as an add-on.

How long do appointments usually take?

Most styles take 2 to 6 hours depending on size, length, and added curls.

Can I request a custom size, length, or color blend?

Absolutely. Use the booking form to describe the look you want and we’ll confirm availability.

Is a deposit required?

Deposits are typically collected once your appointment request is reviewed and approved.
